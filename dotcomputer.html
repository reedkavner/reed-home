Reed Kavner / reed.computer

reed.computer is an unscripted show I created for the TruTV Comedy Breakout Initiative at the New York Televison festival, where it was selected as a finalist in 2018.

Silicon Valley is driven by the thesis that any problem can be solved with technology. reed.computer takes that idea to its logical conclusion and creates a world in which an app, website, or automated contraption is built in response to every problem, no matter how small, specific or unusual. It's Pimp My Ride but with computers and I am Xzibit.
